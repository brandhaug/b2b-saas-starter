A Workspace Role is the permission level a Member has within one Workspace. The starter defines three roles in `workspace_members.role`: owner, admin, member.

> **Enforcement status:** roles are modeled in the schema and surfaced through the `WorkspaceMembership` capability, but route-level enforcement is not yet wired. Every signed-in user currently passes `requireSession` regardless of role — see ARCHITECTURE.md §Authorization model.

## Owner

Exactly one Member per Workspace is the owner. Ownership transfer and owner-only governance Notifications are scaffolded in the data model; the transfer flow itself and the notification routing are not yet implemented.

## Admin

Admins are intended to manage members, Invitations, API Tokens, Webhook Endpoints, billing, and integration configuration. There can be multiple admins.

## Member

Members are intended to read workspace data and operate within the Reference Application without changing configuration. Most users are members.

## Why three, not four

Three is the minimum that supports a real B2B governance model without inventing roles that nothing meaningfully enforces. Adding finer-grained permissions would mean inventing a workspace-level policy language; the starter leaves that as a future module rather than guessing the right shape.
